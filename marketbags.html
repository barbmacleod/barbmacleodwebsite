A few years ago on a very snowy day in February I had an idea to put the image of my paintings on a market bag. The result was these reuable and durable bags.Washable, spacious and functional they were a great improvment on the curious assortment of bags I had been using. As an extra benefit they were images of the places I love and know on PEI. How nice to be carrying around images that are culturally important to me as opposed to displaying someones corporate logo.

This image is of Victoria Row on Richmond Street in Charlottetown, PEI. The Bag is 12"x16" with an 8"gusset. Victoria Row is a fun and festive street that many Islanders and visitors love!
