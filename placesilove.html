Series - Places I Love on PEI

Places I Love on PEI was the first series I developed. As it came together I had an idea to put these paintings on Market Bags. Three of the paintings in this series are available as market bags. I use reusable bags everyday and thought they should be nicer. Now when I'm out doing chores I take a little piece of PEI with me.
