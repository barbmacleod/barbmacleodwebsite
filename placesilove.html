Series - Places I Love on PEI

Places I Love on PEI was the first series I developed. As it came together I had an idea to put these paintings on Market Bags. Three of the paintings in this series are also available as market bags. Its a nice feeling to carry around a reuseable bag that has an artistic image of my home. I was getting a bit tired of advertising for large corporations everytime I went out!
