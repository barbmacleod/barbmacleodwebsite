Series - The Ladies

This series, The Ladies, was inspired by the sketches of David Dowtown, a renowned high fashion artist. Something about the stylized ladies really caught my eye. I decided to recreate them in acrylic. They turned out really well and would be a great visual addition to a beauty salon or spa!
