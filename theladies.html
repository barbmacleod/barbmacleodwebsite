Series - The Ladies

This series, The Ladies, was inspired by the sketches of David Dowtown, a renowned high fashion artist, whom I admire greatly. The love of fashion and fabrics is from my youth. My first career choice was a fashion designer and in fact I did attend a fashion college in Toronto but at the time my practical side dominated and I took the business program. It was this college education which stimulated my career as a speciality retail entrepreneur.
